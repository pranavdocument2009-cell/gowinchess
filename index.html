Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What is the age requirement?
Children aged 5 years and above can join our chess programs. We have specially designed curricula for different age groups.
Are classes online?
Yes, all classes are conducted live online through our interactive virtual classroom platform. Students can learn from the comfort of home.
How many classes are there each month?
We offer 8 classes per month (2 classes per week), with each session lasting 60 minutes. Additional practice sessions are also available.
Is there a free demo class?
Yes, one free demo class is available for all new students. It's a great way to experience our teaching methodology before enrolling.
